RÍO LIMAY
Trout Fly Fishing Experience

Float the legendary Aluminé and Limay rivers in drift boats, wade pristine pools, and cast to wild brown and rainbow trout in waters with virtually zero fishing pressure. Based at Bravo Sur Lodge — five riverside suites, gourmet asado dinners, and Argentine wines poured streamside.

  • Daily float trips on Aluminé, Limay & Juramento rivers
  • Orvis-endorsed professional guides
  • 5 private double rooms overlooking the river
  • All meals, premium Argentine wines & beverages
  • Streamside gourmet lunch over an open firepit
  • Ground transfers from San Martín de los Andes airport
  • Massage service after long fishing days

Santa Fe · Paraná River
GOLDEN
DORADO
The River Tiger Experience

Battle the Salminus brasiliensis — the River Tiger — on the mighty Paraná. Known for explosive surface strikes and acrobatic jumps, the Golden Dorado is South America's most coveted sport fish. Based at Rio Bravo Lodge, just 1 hour from Buenos Aires.

  • Full-day guided Dorado sessions on the Paraná
  • Fly fishing or spin fishing — your choice
  • Deluxe private rooms with en-suite bathrooms
  • All gourmet meals, local wines, beers & spirits
  • Swimming pool & jacuzzi at the lodge
  • Airport transfers from Santa Fe / Paraná
  • Full-time trip coordinator from day of booking

Who we are

YOUR BRIDGE
TO ARGENTINA'S
BEST WATERS

Argentina Flishing Experience is your dedicated partner for booking Argentina's finest sport fishing. We are official resellers of Achala Outfitters — the country's most trusted fishing outfitter, with over 25 years on the water.

Achala Outfitters, led by brothers Dario and Diego Buzurro, holds Orvis-Endorsed Guide status — one of the highest distinctions in fly fishing worldwide. Their team has been guiding anglers on the Aluminé, Limay, Juramento and Paraná rivers since 1998, earning a reputation for exceptional organization, top-tier equipment, and truly memorable days on the water.
